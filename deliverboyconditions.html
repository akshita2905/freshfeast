Delivery Partner Terms & Conditions

  1. Delivery partners must maintain a clean, well-groomed appearance and behave professionally at all times while on duty.
  2. All deliveries should be made promptly at the scheduled time without unnecessary delays to ensure customer satisfaction.
  3. Delivery bags must be clean, insulated, and food-safe. Proper care should be taken to avoid spillage or damage to food items.
  4. Partners must verify the correct order before pickup and cross-check the customer name and address before handing over the delivery.
  5. FreshFeast does not tolerate rude or inappropriate behavior toward customers or chefs. Respectful communication is mandatory.
  6. Delivery partners must not open or tamper with food packages under any circumstance.
  7. A valid driving license, ID proof, and proof of residence are required to register and must be kept updated with the platform.
  8. In case of delays or unforeseen issues during delivery, partners must immediately inform the FreshFeast support team.
  9. Delivery partners must not accept or demand cash or any form of direct payment from customers.
  10. Alcohol, tobacco, or other prohibited substances must not be consumed while on duty.
  11. If a customer refuses a delivery or reports any issues, the partner must follow FreshFeast's return or dispute resolution protocol.
  12. FreshFeast reserves the right to conduct background checks and/or vehicle inspections at any point in time.
  13. Inappropriate behavior, repeated late deliveries, or customer complaints may result in suspension or permanent removal from the platform.
  14. Delivery partners are responsible for maintaining updated vehicle insurance and all legal documentation if using a vehicle for delivery.
  15. FreshFeast may update terms periodically. Delivery partners will be informed, and continued service implies acceptance of the updated terms.
